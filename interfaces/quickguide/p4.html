The window on the left shows the "educator" portal; this is the page that the teacher sees on her laptop. The two windows on the right are "station" portals; these are the pages that appear on the computers that will serve as simulated seismographs.

Click on the "station" button on each of the station portals to select the seismograph represented in that window. (Choose two different numbers.) Select the "schedule" tab on the educator portal; this is the resource that allows the teacher to set up a series of roomquakes for delivery to her classroom. (Times are expressed as Greenwich Mean Time). Build a schedule by Adding events and Saving your schedule, and watch the "station" windows to see schedule delivered. (Quakes should not be scheduled more frequently than one minute apart.)

This configuration of resources can be used to introduce learners to properties of the spatial and temporal distributions of series of earthquakes.
