planning

In order for the simulated seismographs to work properly, the teacher needs to specify the dimensions of her room and the locations of the seismographs. The window here shows the "educator" portal; this is the page that the teacher sees on her laptop. Select the "room" tab on the educator portal; this is the resource that allows the teacher to configure her classroom. Enter the dimensions of the room and the coordinates of the seismographs, then "Save configuration."
